All wonderful classes, maybe light reminder to work on labs/homework.

Intro to Engineering Agenda:
This class does get loud and chaotic at some points.
1. Each group should be working on their Egg Drop project. This means they do get up and work.
2. Students can take from the materials at the front of the classroom as needed. They are limited in how much tape they can use though, maximum of 3 feet.
